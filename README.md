# Card Games

Greetings, Traveler!

For some reason, I've been writing rules for card games. The current lineup:

**Devil Plays Games**

- Final Run: 2 player cooperative. You play as The Fixer and the Specialist. Schnapsen lineage, asymmetric information
- Panther: 3 player competitive, 1v2. Bridge lineage, exposed dummy hand
- Acrobatics: 4 player cooperative. You play as performers in high stakes acrobatics show, with restricted communication. The Crew lineage.
- Secret Agent: 4 player competitive, 1v3. Hidden role. Spades lineage (vaguely).
- Knives: 5 player competitive, 2v3. Hidden partner. Sheepshead lineage.
- The Chair: 6 player competitive. Simultaneous play and hidden agendas. Original mechanics.
